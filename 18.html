Ans:
(i)
Algal bloom refers to the excessive growth of algae in water body due to enrichment of
excessive nutrients in it.
(ii)
The red dinoflagellates undergo rapid multiplication eg. Gonyaulux which make the sea
appear red. It is called red tide.
12.Why are some fungi grouped under “fungi imperfecti”?
Ans:
The fungi commonly reproduce asexually. A part of mycelium is detached by
fragmentation into small pieces which grow into new mycelium but in some fungi sexual
reproduction also takes place. There is a group of fungi which reproduces completely by
asexual spores & not by sexual spores so they are grouped under fungi imperfecti.
13.Explain “Numerical taxonomy”.
Ans:
Numerical taxonomy refers to use of technological methods in taxonomy. The
observable characters are studied. The number & codes are assigned to them for computer
like (+) & (-). The date processed by computer scores the taxa as per number of unit
characters possessed by them.

(ii)

(iii)
Multicellular groups have evolved from the protists.
