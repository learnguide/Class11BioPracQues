6.Describe the plant cell as an osmotic system?
Ans.
The plasma membrane in plant cell with the vacuolar membrane & cytoplasmic film or
alone is more or less semi permeable in mature. This membrane allows the water molecules
to get through it freely whereas it allows certain molecules to enter & prevent others. The
cytoplasm is surrounded by the cell wall. It possesses very much higher concentration than
the solutions entering the plant cells via osmosis. So the plant cell functions as an osmotic
system if it fulfills the following two conditions:-
i) It has a semi-permeable membrane.
ii)It possesses a liquid substance having much higher concentration therefore, plant cell acts
as osmotic system.
