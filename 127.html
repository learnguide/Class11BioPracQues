Cell The Unit of Life
1 Marks Questions
1.Define totipotency?
Ans.
Each vegetative plant cell has capacity to develop into a full plant. This characteristic of
plant is called totipotency.
2.Name two cell organelles which contain their own DNA?
Ans.
Mitochondria & chloroplast.
3.Which cell organelle functions as “seggregation apparatus”?
Ans.
Endoplasmic Reticulum (ER)
4.Which structure is called little nucleus?
Ans.
Nucleolus.
5.What is the function of contractile vacuole?
Ans.
Water balance or osmoregulation.
6.Name the enzymes present in peroxysomes?
Ans.
Catalase & B- hydroxyoxidase.
7.Who gave the statement “Omnis cellular cellula”?
Ans.
Rudolf Virchow.
