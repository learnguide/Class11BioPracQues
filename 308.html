3.Why do giant squids have very thick nerve fiber?
Ans
. The velocity of a nerve impulse in a nerve fiber depends on two factors i. e. on its
myelinated and also on the thickness of the fibers. The impulses travel faster in thicker nerve
fibers since giant squids are very large sized aquatic animals they have thick nerve fibers.
