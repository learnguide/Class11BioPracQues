14.What is inflorescence? What are its two types?
Ans.
The arrangement of flowers an the floral axis is called inflorescence. Inflorescence are
of two major types:-
(a)
Racemose inflorescence:- main axis continues to grow & flowers are borne laterally in
acropetal succession.
(b)
Cymose inflorescence: - main axis terminates in a flower hence, is limited in growth,
flowers are borne in basipetal order.
