ANDROECIUM:- It represents as male reproductive parts. It consists of stamens in each
stamen there are three parts:-
(a)
Anther:- Knob like bilobed structure containing pollen grains. Each lobe contains two
chambers called pollen sac.
(b)
Connective:- A strip of tissue, which connects the anther lobe is called connective.
(c)
Filament:- a slender stalk by which anther lobes are attached is called filament.
(iv).
GYNOECIUM:- It is the female part of the flower it is made up of three parts
(a)
Stigma:- upper part of pistil which receives pollen grains
(b)
Style: - The stalk between stigma & ovary.
(c).
Ovary:- basal part containing ovules.
