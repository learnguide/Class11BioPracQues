2.How are glycosidic bonds formed?
Ans.
The glycosidic or ketone group of a monosaccharide can react & bind with an alcoholic
group of another organic compound to join the two compounds together. This bond is known
as glycosidic bond.
3.What do you mean by steady state?
Ans.
An open system always remains in steady state i.e. the rate of in put of energy & matter
is always equal to the output of energy & matter.
