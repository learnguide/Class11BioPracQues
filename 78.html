18.“Underground parts of a plant are not always roots” justify the statement.
Ans.
Usually roots develop below the ground. But in potato, the stem gets modified into
“tuber” like structure for the storage of reserve food material. These tubers develop & grow
under the ground. Potato is a stem because it bears scale leaves, buds, nodes etc.
