5.Why is closed circulatory system considered advantageous?
Ans.
Closed circulatory system is considered advantageous for the following reasons-
a)
It maintains sufficient high blood pressure, blood flows at a high velocity; this quickens
the supply of needed material and removal of wastes from the tissues.
b)
The volume of blood flowing to a particular organ / tissues can be regulated to the need of
the tissues.
