5.Mention three similarities & three differences between mitochondria & chloroplasts?
Ans.
SIMILARITIES BETWEEN MITOCHONDRIA & CHLOROPLAST
(i)
Mitochondria & chloroplasts are semi-automous organelle & they possess their own DNA,
RNA as well as ribosomes.
(ii)
They both develop & originate in the same way, formed by division of pre-existing
organelle
(iii)
Both of them contain circular DNA.
DIFFERENCES BETWEEN MITOCHONDRIA & CHLOROPLAST
(i)
Mitochondria occurs in all eukaryotic cells while chloroplast are present only in plant
cells.
(ii)
Pigments are absent in mitochondria but always present in chloroplast.
(iii)
The inner membrane of mitochondria are folded into cristae where as cristae are absent
in chloroplast.
