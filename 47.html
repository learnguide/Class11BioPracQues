(i)
Thallophytic:- plant body is thallus i.e. not differentiated into root, stem & leaves eg.
chlorella, ulothrix, spirogyra etc.
(ii)
Bryophyta:- Amphibious in habit, water is necessary for fertilization, Vascular tissues are
absent eg. Riccia, Marchantia, funaria.
(iii)
Pteridophyta:- plant body is differentiated into distinct underground stem like rhizome
bearing roots & aerial shoots with leaves. They are called “primitive vascular plants “ eg.
equistem, Adiantum, pteris
(iv)
Gymnosperm:- seeds are naked eg. cycas, pinus, cedars
(v)
Angiosperm:- seeds are protected inside the friuts eg
.(i)

maize, rice &
(ii).
Dicotyledons eg gram, pea, sunflower.
