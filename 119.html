16.How can a male frog be distinguished from a female frog ?
Ans.
The male frogs may be distinguished by presence of sound producing vocal sacs. They
also has a copulatory pad on the first digit of the forelimbs. Vocal sacs & copulatory pads are
absent in female frogs.
