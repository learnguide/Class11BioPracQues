2.What is mitosis? Give a brief account of mitosis in animal cell?
Ans.
Mitosis is an equational cell division in which number of chromosomes in parent &
progeny cell remains same.
STAGES OF MITOSIS:-
1)PROPHASE:- a)
chromosome material condenses to form compact mitotic chromosomes.
Chromosomes are seen to be composed of two chromatids attached together at centromere.
b)
Initiation of assembly of mitotic spindle, the microtubules the protein components of the
cell cytoplasm help in the process.
2)METAPHASE:- a)
Spindle fibres attach to kinetochores of chromosomes
b)
Chromosomes are moved to spindle equator & get aligned along metaphase plate through
spindle fibres to both poles.
3)ANAPHASE:- a)
centromere splits and chromatids separate
