3.Explain the fluid mosaic model of plasma membrane.
Ans.
The fluid mosaic model was proposed by G.Nicholson & s. singer. According to this each
phospholipids layer is bimolecular & their hydrophilic ends are pointed towards top &
bottom respectively.
In this, proteins are of two categories- peripheral (extrinsic) & integral (intrinsic). The
integral proteins are tightly held in place by strong hydrophilic or hydrophobic interactions
or both and are difficult to remove from the membranes. Two peripheral proteins are
superficially arranged on either side membrane selectively permeable thus this model
explains cell membrane is quasifluid & is made up of “protein icebergs in the sea of lipids”.
