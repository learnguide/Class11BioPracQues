2.Briefly explain the chemisomotic hypothesis?
Ans.
Chemiosmotic hypothesis explained the mechanism of ATP synthesis in chloroplast. In
photosynthesis, ATP synthesis is linked to development of proton gradient across a
membrane. These are membrane of thylakoids. The proton accumulation is towards the
inside of the membrane (in the lumen).
The processes which occur during activation of electrons and their transport to determine
the steps that causes a proton gradient to develop. ATP synthesis is linked to development of
proton gradient.
