Characteristics – PGRs are small, simple molecules of diverse chemical structure

They are broadly divided into two groups based on their function in a living plant body:-
a)
On group of PGR’s involved in growth promoting activities, e.g, cell division, cell
enlargement, pattern formation, tropic growth, flowering, fruiting and seed formation. They
also termed plant growth promoter e.g. auxons, gibberellins, cytokinins.
b)
PGR’s of other group are in plant responses to wounds and stresses of biotic and abiotic
origin. These may be involved in different growth inhibiting activities like dormancy and
abscission; e.g, abscissic acid (ABA). The gaseous PGR is ethylene. It is inhibitor of growth
activities mostly.
