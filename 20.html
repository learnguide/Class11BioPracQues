19. What are insectivorous plants? Give an example.
Ans:
Insectivorous plants are carnivorous plants. They trap insects to supplement nutritional
requirement of nitrogen. These are green plants & their leaves are modified to trap insects to
overcome shortage of nitrogen eg. in pitcher plant (Nepenthes) leaf blade is modified into a
pitcher.
