9.Discuss the structure of haversian system in the histology of bone?
Ans.
Bone consists of connective tissue having matrix surrounded by periosteum. In
mammalian bone haversian canal which carry blood vessels & nerves of the bone are
surrounded by a number of concentric lamellae of intercellular matrix & bone.
