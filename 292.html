9.What makes the synovial joints freely movable? List any four types of synovial joints.
Ans.
Synovial Joints – In the synovial joints not only, a space called synovial cavity is present
between articulating bones.
This cavity is filled with synovial fluid, that reduces the friction on the articulating surface of
bones; so the synovial joints are freely movable.
Synovial joints are of the following types:
(i)
Ball and socket joint
(ii)
Hinge joint
(iii)
Pivot joint.
