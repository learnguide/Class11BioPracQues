Ans.
Advantages of step wise oxidation during respirations-
a)
It facilitates the utilization of a relatively higher proportion of that energy in ATP
synthesis.
b)
Activities of enzymes for the different steps may be enhanced or inhibited by specific
compounds. This provides a means of controlling the rate of the pathway and the energy
output according to the need of the cell.
c)
The same pathway may be utilized for forming intermediates used in the synthesis of
other bimolecular like amino acids.
