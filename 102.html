13. How can you identify a monocot stem and a dicot stem? Give reasons.
Ans.
In monocot stem, the vascular bundles are scattered. No distinction between pitch &
cortex. Cambium is not present. Vascular bundles are closed whereas, dicot stem shows
epidermis, cortex & stele. Epidermis bears appendages-trichomes. The vascular bundles are
open & are arranged in rings. Cortex & pith are distinct cambium present.
