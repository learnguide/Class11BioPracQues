8.Outline the role of coelom in animals.
Ans.
Coelom is the space between body wall & alimentary canal of organisms it is lined by
mesoderm. Visceral organs lie in the coelom. Flatworm does not have coelom. Hence they
are called acoelamata. Pseudocoelom is found in the round worm. Annelids are coelomate
animals.
9.Mention the unique features of phylum mollusca.
Ans. (i)
Body soft as well as unsegmented
(ii)
The body is covered by calcareous shell & mantle.
(iii)
Body is divided into – head, visceral mass &foot.
(iv)
Buccal mass possesses radula.
