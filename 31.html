6. Give some important features of dicots?
Ans.
The dicotyledons are characterized by either woody or herbaceous habit, their flower
parts usually are in four or five their leaves are net-veined, vascular bundles are arranged in
a circle or ring within the stem. The dicots have two cotyledons in their seeds.
7.List four classes of plants belonging to fern group.
Ans.
Pteridophytes or fern group are divided into four classes:-
(i)
Class 1: psilopsida eg. psilotum nudum.
(ii)
Class 2: Lycopsida eg. Lycopodium phlegmaria.
(iii)
Class 3: Sphenopsida eg. Equisetum
(iv)
Class 4: pteropsida eg. Adiantum & pteridium
