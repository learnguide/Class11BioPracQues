2.What are essential mineral elements?
Ans.
Mineral elements found in soil which may enter plants through the roots. More than 60
elements of 105 discovered so far occur in different plants. Some accumulate selenium but
some others gold. Some plants growing near nuclear test sites takes up radioactive
strontium.
