3 Marks Questions
1.What is the significance of meiosis?
Ans.
Significance of Meiosis:-
a)
It reduces number of chromosomes to half in daughter cells.
b)
It is very essential phenomenon in life cycle of sexually reproducing animals as it restores
the fixed number of chromosomes.
c)
Gametes are formed as a result of meiosis. Each gamete possesses half the number of
chromosomes present in somatic cells.
d)
It avoids the multiplication of chromosomes & thus maintains the stability & constant
number of chromosomes of the species.
e)
During the crossing over, exchange of nuclear material, genetic variations within the
species takes place with the result that new combinations of genetic material are formed.
